Bronco Guide 2021

The Bronco Guide Media Kit is an advertisement towards potential sponsors to advertise in the annual Bronco Guide magazine distributed to students on campus. This year the media kit honed into the messages set by the COVID-19 pandemic. The narrative created was for the campus to welcome students from the past into a brighter playful future. I wanted to push the lengths of the title typography to become more dynamic and playful by handwriting the title itself.

PORSCHE

Previous Next

An advertisement set for Porsche, the aim was to find a way to show off the heritage of the brand. The cars are shown next to European masterpieces of our time. The spotlight onto the cars rather than the art show the cars, shows the cars to not be more revered than those masterpieces, but also as art themselves. The style of the advertisements strongly resembled an already established layouts of Porsche ad placements in magazines like Car & Driver.

TROPHY LAUNDROMAT

Previous Next

Trophy Laundromat started as a design that marries the mundane with surrealism. Inspired by a scene in Disney’s “Cinderella,” where Cinderella is washing the laundry, the common yet majestic deer was tasked with the same duty. The oddly humanistic deer was able presented the same eeriness of a contorted taxidermy deer. Yet, the beautiful surrounding scenery hid the deer. The t-shirt aimed to capture the audience by creating a sense of haunting wonder.

MOKUZAI

Previous Next

Mokuzai, or wood, in Japanese is a men’s hygiene brand. The brand exemplifies the stoic strength of towering timber. This was achieved through the minimalism of the packaging. Strong saturation of color combined with structured geometric shapes and sans serif text. A single stripe of color gives the user an easy differentiation between scents when the item is displayed on shelves or in a webpage.
