I learned early on in my childhood the joys of art and beauty can bring to the people. I constantly sought new techniques to create art. In the processes of discovery, I grew through the struggles of failure and adaptation. College led to my discovery of graphic design, where I learned the joy of storytelling, communication and design. Today, I continuously strive to create work that breaks away from fleeting trends to build a lasting design. This mission is united with my love for studying the language and drive of a brand. In my free time, I visit art museums and daydream about Porsche 911 and Taycan.
