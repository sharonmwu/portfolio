TURBO WEENIE RACE

Brand Identity : Student Work

Objective

To create a cohesive brand identity, promotional material and collateral. It seeks to create a fun and whimsical image for the celebration of the dachshund breed.

Who is Turbo Weenie Race?

A one day fun charity dachshund dog race. Aims to bring family joy with dogs and food trucks. The winner of the race will be able to donate a $5000 reward to an animal shelter of their choice along with gifts from the race brand sponsors.

Target Audience

Children, Familes, Dog - Lovers, Dog Hobby Enthusiasts

Keywords:

Fun, Whimsicals. Cartoony, Modern
